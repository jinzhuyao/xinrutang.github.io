About

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Department of Informatics, University of California, Irvine. I am advised by Dr. Anne Marie Piper as a member of Accessibility Research Collective. Outside school, I volunteer with AImpower.org to co-develop more accessible speech technologies with and for the stuttering community. I was also fourtunate to work with Dr. Yixin Zou in her Human-Centered Security and Privacy group at Max Planck Institute for Security and Privacy (MPI-SP).

Research Interests

  • Universal quantum control theory for discrete- and continuous-variable systems governed by time-dependent Hermitian or non-Hermitian Hamiltonian.
  • Exotic applications based on the control theory, such as state transfer, entanglement generation, geometric quantum gates and braiding of Majorana zero modes.
  • Quantum phenomenon such as quantum blockade or magnon blockade.
